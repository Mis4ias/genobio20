Registration fees are detailed in the table below. On-line registration will be accepted until April 10th, 2020. On-line early registration must accompany fee payment. Your registration will be acknowledged by e-mail. Thereafter, you must register on site.

Fees

Registration to the Congress includes access to all:
  • Sessions;
  • Coffee breaks;
  • Happy hour.
In addition, the registered participant will:
  • Receive the Congress Brochure with the detailed program, on site;
  • Have access to the printable Certificate of Participation, through the website;
  • Have access to the printable Certificate of Study Presentation (for presenting authors), through the website;

Cancellation and reimbursement

In case there is formal notice of cancellation by participants, an e-mail must be sent to with a reimbursement request up to 30 (thirdy) days prior to event opening. Reimbursement will be made up to 45 days after the event and the 50% of the paid amount will be reimbursed.

Important note: No refunds will be provided in case of missing the event.
